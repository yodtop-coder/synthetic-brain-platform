# synthetic-brain-platform
"AI-powered Competency Development &amp; Operational Readiness Platform for the Refinery &amp; Petrochemical industry, 
featuring the 'OpsTwin-ครูพี่เชี่ยวชาญ' GenAI mentor."

Project Name: Synthetic Brain Platform

Overview:
Synthetic Brain is a specialized Digital Transformation Platform for the Refinery and Petrochemical sector. It integrates Generative AI (Google Gemini), IK-Q AI platform with traditional Competency Management Systems (CMS) to accelerate operator readiness and reduce human error. The core of the system is "OpsTwin-ครูพี่เชี่ยวชาญ," an AI mentor capable of simulating expert personas (Panel Operator, Safety Expert, POM) to guide users through complex operational scenarios.

Key Features:
•	OpsTwin AI Mentor: Context-aware chat assistant with RAG capabilities for SOPs and Work Instructions.
•	Advanced Analytics: Dashboards for OTS (Operator Training Simulator) scoring, uptime analysis, and competency gaps (AG1-AG3).
•	Development Roadmap: Digitalized Base Progression Scheme, Competency Assessment Result, Individual Development Plans (IDP) linked to 7 Learning Solutions (7LS).
•	Operational Readiness:  Tracking of Panel Man readiness and Panel Man list Management.

Tech Stack: React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Recharts, Google GenAI SDK.
